About me

I am an orthodontist and love straightening teeth. In my practice, I have had to learn new technologies that have become available and have tried to adapt myself to all those changes. Adapting to changes can be difficult. Learning new things can aslo be very difficult. But the best way to deal with them is to face them. I finally decided to face one of my biggest fears and that was coding. Learning to do things on the black screen of git gives me so much joy! and it is very difficult at times, but I am ready to tackle this.
